Bill Gates - Founder of Microsoft

    Here is the timeline of Bill Gates's life:

  • 1955 - Born in Seattle, Washington, USA.
  • 1967 - Enrolled in the Lakeside School.
  • 1973 - Entered Harvard as a pre-law major, but soon shifts focus.
  • 1977 - Set up Microsoft in Albunquerque, N.M.
  • 1987 - Becomes youngest billionare ever.
  • 2000 - Steps down as CEO of Microsoft.
  • 2007 - "Graduates" from Harvard.
  • 2008 - Though his fortune continues to grow, Gates is no longer the richest man on the planet.
